# 🛠️ KALUXHOST | PREMIUM BOT COMMAND GUIDE (2026)



> **Note:** This guide covers all modules currently active on your VPS (**140.245.232.60**). All data is persistent and stored in `kaluxhost.db`.



---



### 🗳️ MODULE: SUGGESTION BOX (#19)

*Engage the community with an interactive, one-vote-per-user system.*

*   `!setsuggest` — Sets the current channel as the destination for suggestions.

*   `!suggest <text>` — Submits a suggestion. Creates an embed with **Upvote/Downvote** buttons and real-time counters.

*   *Anti-Flood:* Users are limited to **1 suggestion per 5 minutes**.



---



### 🎭 MODULE: DYNAMIC ROLE MENUS (#17)

*Customizable button menus for Notifications, Colors, or Game Roles.*

*   `!rolemenu "Title" #Hex @Role1 @Role2...` — Creates a button-based menu.

    *   **Example:** `!rolemenu "Notifications" #00FF00 @Announcements @Giveaways`

*   *Feature:* **Toggle Logic** (Click once to add role, click again to remove).



---



### ⭐ MODULE: VERIFIED REVIEWS (#14)

*Build trust with a star-rated vouch system for your hosting services.*

*   `!setvouch` — Sets the channel where public 5-star reviews will be posted.

*   `!vouch <1-5> <comment>` — Posts a gold-themed verified review with your logo.

*   `!vouchstats` — Shows your overall **Trust Score** (Average Rating & Total Vouches).



---



### 🎁 MODULE: GIVEAWAY MANAGER (#15)

*Professional giveaways with live entry tracking and countdowns.*

*   `!gstart <time> <winners> <prize>` — Starts a giveaway.

    *   **Time Units:** `s` (sec), `m` (min), `h` (hours), `d` (days).

    *   **Example:** `!gstart 2h 1 10GB VPS Node`

*   *Feature:* **Live Entry Counter** updates the embed every time a user joins.



---



### 📌 MODULE: STICKY EMBEDS & POLLS

*Keep important info visible and gather quick opinions.*

*   `!stickembed <text>` — Keeps a branded embed at the bottom of the chat.

*   `!stickstop` — Disables the sticky in the current channel.

*   `!stickpoll yesno <Question>` — Simple Yes/No/Maybe button poll.

*   `!stickpoll multi <Q, A, B, C>` — Multiple choice button poll (up to 7 options).
